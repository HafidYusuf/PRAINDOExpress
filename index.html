How It Works?

  • Sender Hand Over the Package to PRAINDO Express

    What to do
    Pengirim WAJIB mencantumkan data diri lengkap penerima di negara tujuan, yaitu Nama Lengkap, Alamat lengkap negara tujuan, No. telepon atau No. Handphone penerima, dan isi barang beserta jumlah barang dalam satu paket yang akan dikirim. (Contoh : Nama : Mohammad Arsyad, Alamat : Jln. Merbah Pusat, Bayan Lepas, Malaysia, No. Handphone : +6010 XXX XXXX , Isi Barang : Kosmetik (3 pcs : toner, cream, soap)

  • The Package will be Weighed and We will measure the Volume (L x W x H : 5000)

  • Praindo Will Give a Shipping Tracking Number After the Customer Has Paid the Payment

  • Package Will Be Processed Immediately, and Sent To the Destination Country

    Destination Branch of Picking Up Goods(Specifically for HONGKONG Country)
    Khusus negara HONGKONG, pembayaran akan dilakukan di cabang tujuan HONGKONG, berikut kami lampirkan cabang tujuan pengambilan barang :
    Causeway Bay, Mongkok, Sham Shui Po, Tsuen Wan, Yuen Long, Tuen Mun, Wong Tai Sin, Choi Hung, Kwuntong, Taipo, Polam, Wham Poa, Jordan, Fan Ling, Ma On Shan, Tsing Yi, Shatin, Macau
